Mission Launch - 1953

Space Foods Company had a failed mission launch in 1953 when the home soda syrup manufacturer went bankrupt before bringing it's product to market. Our goal is to find success in the company's second launch and help tell this untold story through our art and even shed some light on the physical antique bottles that have collected dust while eagerly waiting for another chance to complete the mission.
The original company left behind thousands of these cool collectible glass bottles for us to discover in our warehouse. Every bottle has its own rank, color, and flavor cap some being more rare than others. We want to being these antique collectible items to the digital world via the NFT market.
