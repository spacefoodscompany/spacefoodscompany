1,953 Crew Members

Space Foods Company was an American business located in Baltimore, Maryland. An idea before it's time, the company developed a line called Galaxy Home Syrups...for Soft Drinks. The homemade soda kit collection consisted of 10 different ranks of the crew members with 8 different colors. The bottles also had a screw-on cap with flavor label. The five flavors were Cherry, Raspberry, Lemon-lime, Grape and Orange. These awesome bottles were put into production and boxed in cases of 12. We don't know when the company planned to launch these kits to the public, but in 1953 Space Foods Company filed for bankruptcy and deemed the initial launch a failure.

In 1971, our Space Ace's grandfather purchased a building for his manufacturing company. To his surprise, the building came with more than just factory floor space. The upstairs was home to thousands of these boxed crew members, who never had the chance to complete their launch. Space Ace's family has sold a few to collectors, but never found a way to tell the story of these bottles. Until now. Now, Space Foods Company has the opportunity to tell the story of these unique antique bottles. A story that we will tell together as a community through our art inspired by the physical bottles themselves!

Prelude

Not long after the end of WWII, The Space Race kicked off between the U.S. and Soviet Russia - a competition between two powerful nations to build a superior technology strong enough for space travel capabilities. Many great achievements emerged from this race starting with the launch of Sputnik in ’57, to Apollo 11 landing on the moon in ’69. As momentous as these achievements were at the time, there was something far more intriguing conspiring several years before…

Our story begins in 1947, the year a UFO crash landed in Roswell, New Mexico. For the last 69 years, the events that transpired the day of the crash have been strongly kept secret. Until now…

Now we will finish our story starting in 1953 when Space Foods Company mysteriously halted it’s operation. As a community we will complete this untold story through a series of writing and art competitions. The winner for each chapter will win a variety of prizes from physical bottles, unique merch and even 1 of 1 NFTs. We will start the competition once we have grown our community to 1,953 prospective crew members so please help us recruit those you would like to travel the cosmos with.

Owning a Space Foods NFT makes you a member of our crew. As a member you will have access to certain channels within our community where you can share your creative ideas and be given the opportunity to win challenges.

04

What is an NFT?

An NFT, or "non-fungible token" can be described as a modern-day collectible. An NFT is a digital asset that links ownership to unique physical or digital items, such as art, real estate, music, etc.
